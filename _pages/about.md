I am a Master's student in Information Networking (Thesis Track) at **Carnegie Mellon University**. My current study specializes in Computer Systems, with a core interest in **Machine Learning Systems**, specifically in optimizing LLM Serving speed and scalability. I am fortunate to be advised by [Prof. Zhihao Jia](https://www.cs.cmu.edu/~zhihaoj2/) for my Master's thesis, and I expect to graduate in Fall 2026.

Before my interest expanded to MLSys, I've had a strong interest in Database Systems and actively sought practical experience. I did two internships at **Google**, respectively in the Core Data Indexing Engine, and GPay Data Platform teams, and another at **Confluent**, where I contributed to the ksqlDB infrastructure in Quotas Service.

I obtained my bachelor's degree in Computer Science from **Hong Kong University of Science and Technology**, where I did a [Final Year Project](https://github.com/DL-for-CFD/alpinn_for_submission) on a fun application of Adversarial Learning, and participated in programming contests (ICPC, OI) and enjoy theoretical topics.
